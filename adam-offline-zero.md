## 🌌 Overview

**ADAM OFFLINE** is a tactical space simulation RPG set in a star cluster isolated by the collapse of the ADAM Gate Network. As a stranded pilot ("Capsuleer"), you must navigate hostile space, mine resources, trade goods, and upgrade your ship to survive.

---

## 🕹️ Controls & Interface

The game is played entirely through a tactical interface. You do not fly the ship directly with a joystick; you issue commands to the ship's computer.

### 🖱️ Mouse Controls
- **Left Click (Space):** Select an object.
- **Left Click + Drag:** Rotate camera.
- **Scroll Wheel:** Zoom in/out.

### 🖥️ HUD Elements
1.  **Overview (Right Panel):** List of all nearby objects sorted by distance.
2.  **Action Menu (Top Right):** Context-sensitive commands for your current target (Approach, Orbit, Warp, Dock, etc.).
3.  **Telemetry (Bottom Center):** Your ship's vital stats (Shield, Armor, Hull, Capacitor) and active modules.
4.  **Module Bar:** Click these buttons or press `F1`, `F2`, `F3` (future) to activate weapons and devices.

---

## ⚔️ Combat Mechanics

Combat is tactical and stat-based.

1.  **Lock Target:** Click an enemy in the overview or 3D view.
2.  **Range:** Ensure you are within range of your weapons.
    *   *Missiles:* Long range, travel time.
    *   *Lasers:* Instant hit, medium range.
3.  **Activate Weapons:** Click the module icon in the bottom bar.
4.  **Defense:** Monitor your shields. If they fail, armor takes damage. If hull reaches 0, your ship explodes!

### 💥 Death Penalty
If your ship is destroyed, your **Escape Pod** will automatically warp you to the nearest friendly station (Haven). You will be given a basic rookie ship, but any cargo you were carrying is **lost forever** in the wreck of your old ship.

---

## ⛏️ Mining & Industry

1.  **Find Ore:** Warp to an **Asteroid Belt**.
2.  **Approach:** Get within 5,000m of an asteroid.
3.  **Lock & Mine:** Target the asteroid and activate your **Mining Laser**.
4.  **Depletion:** Asteroids have finite resources. Once depleted, they vanish.
5.  **Profit:** Return to a station to sell your ore or refine it.

---

## 🚀 Navigation

-   **Sub-Warp:** Used for combat and maneuvering (0-500 m/s).
-   **Warp Drive:** Used to travel between planets and belts within a system (AU/s). Requires a target > 5,000m away.
-   **Jump Gates:** Massive structures that connect different star systems. You must approach within 2,000m to activate them.

### 🗺️ The Cluster

*   **HAVEN (High Sec):** Safe. Police patrols. Best market prices for buying.
*   **FRONTIER (Low Sec):** The wild west. richer ores, but no guards.
*   **DEADZONE (Null Sec):** Lawless pirate territory. Extremely dangerous. The rarest ores are found here.

---

## 📈 Progression

*   **XP:** Earn XP by mining, killing pirates, and trading.
*   **Skills:** Dock at a station to train skills. Skills improve your ship's CPU, Power Grid, Damage, and Mining Yield.
*   **Ships:** Unlock new hulls like the **Prospector Barge** or the classified **Pathfinder Corvette** by gaining XP.

---

## ⚠️ Tips for Rookies

*   Don't fly what you can't afford to lose.
*   Pirates in the Deadzone will **warp scramble** you, preventing escape.
*   Keep your distance! Use "Orbit" to stay moving. A stationary ship is a dead ship.
*   Watch your Capacitor. Running all modules at once will drain your power.

**Good luck, Pilot. The void awaits.**
